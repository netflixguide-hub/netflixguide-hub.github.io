Supported Netflix Devices

Netflix works on thousands of internet-connected devices. Below are the most popular devices that support Netflix sign-in and activation.

Popular Compatible Devices

Smart TVs

Samsung, LG, Sony, TCL, Hisense, Panasonic, Philips and many Android TVs.

Streaming Devices

Roku, Amazon Fire TV Stick, Chromecast, Apple TV and Google TV.

Gaming Consoles

PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X and Series S.

Mobile Devices

Android phones, Android tablets, iPhone and iPad.

Computers

Windows PCs, Mac computers and Chromebooks using a supported web browser.

Set-Top Boxes

Select cable boxes and internet TV receivers include the Netflix app.

Minimum Requirements

  • Stable internet connection.
  • Latest version of the Netflix app.
  • A valid Netflix account.
  • Device software updated to the latest version.

Can't Find Netflix on Your Device?

If your device doesn't include the Netflix app, check for software updates or visit your device manufacturer's app store. Some older devices may no longer support the latest version of Netflix.
