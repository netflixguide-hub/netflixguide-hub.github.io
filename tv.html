Netflix TV Code Guide

Learn where to find your Netflix TV sign-in code and how to enter it successfully to activate your device.

Where Do I Find My Netflix TV Code?

Open the Netflix app on your Smart TV or streaming device and choose Sign In. A unique activation code will appear on your television screen.

Your TV Screen Will Display Something Like:

ABCD-1234

This is an example only. Your code will be different.

How to Use the TV Code

  1. Open the Netflix app on your TV.
  2. Select Sign In.
  3. Wait for the TV code to appear.
  4. Visit the official Netflix activation page on your phone or computer.
  5. Sign in to your Netflix account.
  6. Enter the TV code exactly as shown.
  7. Your TV will automatically sign in.

Why Doesn't My TV Code Work?

  • The code has expired.
  • The code was entered incorrectly.
  • Your TV isn't connected to the internet.
  • The Netflix app needs to be restarted.
  • Your device needs a software update.

Helpful Tips

  • Always enter the code before it expires.
  • Refresh the code if it becomes invalid.
  • Keep your Netflix app updated.
  • Restart your TV if the activation screen doesn't appear.
