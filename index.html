Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate Netflix on my TV?

Open Netflix, sign in, follow the on-screen instructions, and complete the activation process from your browser.


Why isn't my TV code working?

Expired codes, network issues, or signing into the wrong account are common reasons.


Which devices support Netflix?

Most Smart TVs, streaming devices, gaming consoles, and media players support Netflix.
