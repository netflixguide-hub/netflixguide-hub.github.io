Activate Netflix on Your Smart TV

Follow these simple steps to connect your TV with your Netflix account using the TV activation code.

How to Activate Netflix

Step 1

Open the Netflix app on your Smart TV or streaming device.

Step 2

Select Sign In. A TV activation code will appear on your screen.

Step 3

On your phone or computer, open the official Netflix activation page.

Step 4

Sign in with your Netflix account if prompted.

Step 5

Enter the TV code exactly as displayed on your television.

Step 6

Your TV will refresh automatically and your Netflix account will be connected.

Activation Tips

  • Make sure your TV is connected to the internet.
  • Enter the code before it expires.
  • Refresh the code if it becomes invalid.
  • Restart the Netflix app if activation fails.
